  • Sate Lilit

    Sate Lilit is a traditional Bali dish made with fish, rice, and vegetables. It's a hearty meal that tastes like a bowl of chicken with rice.

  • Betutu Duck

    Betutu Duck is a popular dish in Bali. It's made from duck bones, diced fish, and various vegetables. It's a delicious and simple meal that tastes like a dish of fish with rice.

  • Spit Roast Pig Bali style

    Spit Roast Pig is a popular dish in Bali. It's made from pig bones, roasted meat, and various vegetables. It's a delicious and simple meal that tastes like a dish of pig with rice.
